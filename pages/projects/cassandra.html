Cassandra

Cassandra attempts to give more equal access to information and educational recourses for all. Cassandra was desgined to be a hub for free online learning with the hope that it will make sorting through and finding quality educational recources easier for those looking. We live in a time where the cost of quality education is so high that not many people can afford it, leading to institutional class devides and unequal opportunity to jobs and hence a system which makes it extremely hard for individuals to break out of the income brackets that they were born into. Cassandra is aimed to be a non-profit website and will strive to keep only completely free recources being advertised, whilst the users themselves will evaluate the credibility and usefulness of these courses through the voting system.
