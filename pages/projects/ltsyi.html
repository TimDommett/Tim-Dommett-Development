Love The Skin You're In

As tattoos have become less tabboo over the last few decates, there are more people searching for talented tattoo artists and quality tattoo studios to make sure they get the best possible artwork for their life long committment. LTSYI has made it easier than ever to search for studios and to see what other peoples experiences were like with these artists. It has also made it easier for the extremely talented solo-artists to advertise their work, as many of the best artists in the world run smaller businesses and don't have their own websites or marketing campaigns and this is where LTSYI comes in. It is extremely easy for artists to advertise their shops on a platform where it will be seen amoungst all the other artists in their area.
